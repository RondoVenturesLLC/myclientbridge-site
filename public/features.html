Features

What ClientBridge
does for you.

Three capabilities live today. Each one is built around an outcome that matters in real estate — not a software category.

Feature 01

Capture every lead, even the ones from a parking lot conversation.

Met someone at an open house? Got a referral over coffee? Just describe the conversation in your own words. ClientBridge turns that into a structured lead profile, drafts your follow-up message, and saves the whole record to your secure database — automatically.

No forms. No required fields. No friction between meeting someone and capturing them.

Feature 02

Never forget a follow-up.

Log a client update in seconds — what happened, what changed, what's next. ClientBridge summarizes the situation, updates the client record, and drafts the next message for you to review and send.

The blank screen is gone. So is the mental load of remembering what stage every client is in.

Feature 03

See your day at a glance.

Open ClientBridge in the morning and see exactly who needs your attention, what's time-sensitive, and where every active client stands — organized by urgency, surfaced by AI, all in one view.

No hunting through a CRM. No scrolling through email. Just the day in front of you, prioritized.
