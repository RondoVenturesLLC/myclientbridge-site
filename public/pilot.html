What pilot agents get

New Lead Capture, Client Updates, and your Morning Dashboard — live and running from day one. No stripped-down demo version.

What We Ask

We're looking for agents who are actually going to use it.

The pilot only works if real agents are using ClientBridge on real deals. We're not looking for a massive group — we're looking for the right one.

Share honest feedback — what's confusing, what's slow, what you'd change.

Be available for a short check-in conversation once a month (30 minutes max).
